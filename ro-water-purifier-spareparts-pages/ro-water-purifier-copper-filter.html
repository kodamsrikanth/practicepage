Single Copper Filter – Questions & Answers

1. What is a Single Copper Filter?

A. A Single Copper Filter is an inline RO filter that enriches drinking water with copper minerals.

• Improves water taste
• Helps maintain hygienic water

2. Why is a Copper Filter used in RO water purifiers?

A. RO purification removes many natural minerals, including copper.

• Copper filter adds essential copper back to water
• Enhances drinking water quality

3. Where is the Single Copper Filter installed?

A. It is installed in the post-filter stage of the RO system.

• After RO membrane
• Before storage tank

4. How long does a Single Copper Filter last?

A. The lifespan depends on water usage and quality.

• Usually lasts 6 to 12 months
• Timely replacement ensures best results

5. Is the Copper Filter safe for daily drinking water?

A. Yes, it is safe when used as recommended.

• Made with food-grade copper media
• Designed for domestic use

6. Does the Copper Filter change TDS levels?

A. No, it does not significantly alter TDS.

• Adds trace copper minerals only
• Does not affect RO purification

7. Is this Copper Filter suitable for all RO models?

A. Yes, it is compatible with most domestic RO systems.

• Works with RO, UV & UF purifiers
• Standard inline fitting
