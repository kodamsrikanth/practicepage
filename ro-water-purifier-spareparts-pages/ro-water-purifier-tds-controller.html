About RO Booster Pump

1) RO Booster Pump is an essential component used in RO Water Purifiers to increase inlet water pressure.
2) It is mainly required when water pressure is low or when borewell and tanker water is used.
3) The booster pump helps the RO membrane work efficiently and improves water flow rate.
4) This 24V DC booster pump is suitable for 75 GPD, 80 GPD, and 100 GPD RO membranes.
5) It ensures proper rejection of TDS and increases purified water output.
6) The pump is made with a durable diaphragm system for long life and stable performance.
7) It works with SMPS (24V DC) and consumes low electricity.
8) RO Booster Pumps help prevent membrane damage caused by insufficient pressure.
9) Suitable for domestic RO water purifiers used in homes, shops, and small offices.
10) Regular maintenance of the booster pump ensures smooth RO performance and better water quality.

Question and Answers

1. What is RO water?

A. RO water is purified water made using Reverse Osmosis technology.

• Removes high TDS, chemicals, and heavy metals
• Makes water safe for drinking

2. Why is RO water purifier required?

A. RO water purifier is required when water has high TDS or contamination.

• Borewell water
• Hard water areas
• Poor taste or smell in water

3. How often should RO be serviced?

A. RO water purifier should be serviced regularly.

• Every 3 to 6 months service recommended
• Improves performance and water quality

4. When should RO filters be changed?

A. RO filters must be replaced on time.

• Sediment & carbon filters: 6–12 months
• Depends on water quality and usage

5. How long does an RO membrane last?

A. RO membrane life depends on maintenance.

• Usually lasts 2 to 3 years
• Hard water reduces membrane life

6. Do you provide doorstep RO service?

A. Yes, we provide doorstep RO service.

• Home service available
• Quick repair and filter replacement

7. Do you sell RO spare parts?

• Genuine parts for all brands
